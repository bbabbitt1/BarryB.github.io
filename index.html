Projects

Cyclistic Bike Share

In the Google Data Analyst Certificate, I selected the case study "Cyclistic Bike Share" as my Capstone project. As a junior data analyst, I worked in the marketing analyst team at Cyclistic, a bike-share company based in Chicago. Our primary objective was to maximize the number of annual memberships, as the director of marketing believed it was crucial for the company's future success. To achieve this, my team aimed to uncover the key differences in the usage patterns of casual riders and annual members. By gaining valuable insights from the data, we planned to design a new marketing strategy focused on converting casual riders into annual members. However, in order to gain approval from Cyclistic executives, our recommendations needed to be supported by compelling data insights and professional data visualizations.
